### Implementation Approach

Before writing any code, work through each step explicitly:
1. What exactly needs to change and why (based on the snapshot)?
2. Which files are affected and in what order?
3. What existing tests or contracts might break, and how to preserve them?

Ground implementation in the Context Snapshot and loaded project skills. Use numbered steps for multi-file changes. State "First... Then... Finally..." when sequencing edits across files.
